Welcome to ICCSE-5!

We are delighted to welcome you to the 5th International Conference on Computational Sciences and Engineering (ICCSE-5), which will be held in the vibrant city of Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. This premier event brings together researchers, engineers, and industry professionals from around the world to exchange ideas, present cutting-edge research, and explore emerging trends in computational sciences and engineering.

Join us in this dynamic setting to foster collaboration, innovation, and discovery at the forefront of science and technology. We look forward to seeing you in Ho Chi Minh City!

Main theme

1. Computational Material Sciences

- Simulation and Modeling in Chemistry, Materials and Biomocular systems.

2. Data Science and Analytics

- Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence Applications

- Cloud Computing and Big Data Infrastructure

3. Biomedical Engineering and Bioinformatics Innovation

- Medical Imaging & Diagnostics

- Bioinformatics and Systems Biology
