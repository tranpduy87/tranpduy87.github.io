Travel

Travel Guide to Ho Chi Minh City and HCMUT

Arriving in Ho Chi Minh City

Ho Chi Minh City (Saigon) is served by Tan Son Nhat International Airport (SGN), located about 6 km from the city center. Upon arrival, travelers can use the following transport options:

  • Taxi: Available outside the terminal. XanhSM, Vinasun and Mai Linh are reliable options. The fare to central areas is typically around 150,000–250,000 VND. You will need to proceed to the outside area in front of the terminal to line-up for the taxi.
  • Grab: Ride-hailing app that is popular and often cheaper than taxis.
  • Bus: Bus #152 and #109 provide cheap connections to the city center.

Getting to Ho Chi Minh City University of Technology (HCMUT)

HCMUT is located at 268 Ly Thuong Kiet Street, District 10. Depending on your location in Ho Chi Minh City, you can reach the university by:

  • Taxi or Grab: The most convenient way to reach the university from anywhere in the city.
  • Bus: Multiple bus routes serve District 10. Nearby bus stops include those along Ly Thuong Kiet and Su Van Hanh streets.
  • Motorbike rental: A common option in Vietnam for short-term stays; available via rental shops or delivery apps.

Tips for Visitors

  • Have Vietnamese Dong (VND) ready for small expenses, especially for local transport or street food.
  • Use translation apps if you are not fluent in Vietnamese. English is spoken at major hotels but less so elsewhere.
  • Districts 1, 3, and 10 are convenient and safe areas for accommodation close to HCMUT.
  • Be mindful of traffic when crossing streets – local custom is to walk slowly and predictably.
